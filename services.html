Financial Modelling
Through the implementation of financial models, we create a speculative representation or model of a project/company's financial situation and use it to visualize it's current financial position and predict future cash flows. We use accounting data, financial data, and business metrics to create such models. Our model assumptions are based on true or historical data. Models employed include:
  • Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) based on the NPV
  • Sensitivity Analysis
  • Three Statement Models
